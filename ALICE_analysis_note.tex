\begin{abstract}

The production of prompt \Dstar in pp collisions at \s 13 TeV at the LHC are reported in this analysis note using the full data sample collected in 2016, 2017, and 2018. The production cross-section is measured at mid-rapidity as a function of \pt. The \Dstar mesons were reconstructed using the decay channel \Dstar $\rightarrow$ \Dzero \pip, exploiting the \Dzero decay \Dzero $\rightarrow$ $\rm K^-$ \pip. The \Dstar mesons are reconstructed in the range 1 $<$ \pt $<$ 50 GeV/$c$. The \pt -differential cross sections are compared with QCD calculations.

The calculation is performed in two centrality classes, 0--10$\%$ and 30--50$\%$.
